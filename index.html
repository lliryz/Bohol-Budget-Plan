Trip Itinerary

Day-by-day plan with estimated times. All times approximate.

5
Jun · Day 1
Cebu → Tagbilaran → Panglao
🚢
9:00 AM
Take 9AM Ferry — Cebu → Tagbilaran Key Departure
Fast craft departs at 9AM, arrives Tagbilaran in roughly 2 hours.
⚠️ Boat fares are personal — not part of the group budget. Be at the port with time to spare.
⚓
~11:00 AM
Arrive — Tagbilaran Port
Disembark at Tagbilaran Port. From here, head over to Panglao — no rush, the afternoon is open.
🍽️
11:00 AM – 3:00 PM
Lunch + Suroy-suroy Panglao Chill Mode
Lunch anywhere along the way, then suroy-suroy — beaches, side streets, random food spots. No fixed agenda, just get a feel for the place before settling in.
🏡
~3:00 PM
Airbnb Check-in + Panglao Beach Already Booked
Drop bags at the Airbnb, then head straight to the beach. Swim, sit, explore Alona Beach — the whole afternoon is free.
📍 Confirm check-in time with the host. Suggested checkout on Day 2 is 11AM.
🌙
Evening
Dinner + Free Time Mostly Free
Dinner at Airbnb. First night in Bohol. Whatever the group feels like.
🌙 Overnight — Panglao Airbnb
6
Jun · Day 2
Brunch → Checkout → Rental → Danao → Loay
🍳
Morning
Wake Up + Brunch
No alarm. Wake up naturally, have brunch near the Airbnb, and take it easy before checking out.
🏡
~11:00 AM
Airbnb Checkout Suggested 11AM
Pack up and settle with the host. Aiming for 11AM gives enough buffer to pick up the rental and still reach Danao by early afternoon.
🚗
~11:00 AM – 12:00 NN
Pick Up Car Rental + Drive to Danao
Pick up the rental, load the bags, and head north. Danao is roughly 2 to 2.5 hours from Panglao.
🚗 Coordinate rental pickup time in advance. 2-day rental: June 6–7, returned on Day 4.
🎢
2:00 PM – 6:00 PM
Danao Adventure Park Main Activity
The main event — The Plunge. Spend the afternoon at the park, explore the grounds after the jump. See budget tab for fees.
💡 Confirm slot availability at the counter upon arrival. Go straight there first before anything else.
🏘️
6:00 PM onwards
Drive South to Loay Free Stay
Head south to Loay after the park. Dinner somewhere along the drive — it's about 2 hours down. Early sleep; Day 3 starts at 3AM.
🌙 Overnight — Loay (Friend's House) · Free
7
Jun · Day 3
Loay → Alicia → Anda → Cave/Falls → Loay
🌙
3:00 AM
Departure from Loay 3AM Start
Very early departure from Loay. Roughly 2 hours to Alicia — arriving just before dawn beats both the heat and the crowd.
⏰ Sleep early the night before. Water, snacks, and hiking shoes should be ready to go.
⛰️
5:00 AM – 9:00 AM
Hike — Alicia Panoramic Park
Arrive at 5AM and start the ascent. Sunrise from the summit is the reward — panoramic views of Bohol's hills and coastline. Budget around 4 hours for the full hike.
🥾 Expect low visibility on the first stretch of the trail; careful footing is necessary due to slippery sections.
🏖️
10:00 AM – 4:00 PM
Anda Beach
White sand, crystal water. Full beach day — lunch here, swim, nap, recharge after the hike. See budget tab for fees.
🌊
4:00 PM – 6:00 PM
Cave / Falls near Anda Optional
Quick stop at a cave pool or falls near Anda — Cabagnow Cave Pool is a good option. Skip this if the group is wiped out from the beach.
📍 Confirm the exact spot the night before — there are a few options around Anda.
🏘️
6:00 PM onwards
Return to Loay Free Stay
Drive back south to Loay. Dinner on the way. Pack up that night — last morning tomorrow.
🌙 Overnight — Loay (Friend's House) · Free
8
Jun · Day 4
Return Rental → Tagbilaran → Go Home
🌄
Morning
Wake Up — Pack Up at Loay
Last morning in Bohol. Settle any remaining shared expenses, have breakfast, and say goodbye to the house.
🚗
~9:00 AM – 10:00 AM
Return Car Rental Return On Time
Drive to Tagbilaran for the rental drop-off. Car wash before return if the agreement requires it.
🚗 Confirm the return deadline with the rental provider ahead of time — needs to line up with the ferry schedule.
🍽️
~10:00 AM – 12:00 NN
Last Meal in Tagbilaran
Last group meal near the port. One final sit-down before everyone heads home.
🚢
Afternoon
Ferry Back to Cebu — Go Home 🎉
Ferry back to Cebu. Settle any remaining shared costs on the boat. Trip's a wrap.
🎉 Boat fares are personal — not in the group budget. Coordinate ferry schedules ahead of the trip.
